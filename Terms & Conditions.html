Terms and Conditions

Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Punctuality: All employees are expected to strictly adhere to their scheduled IN and OUT times. Lateness without prior approval should be avoided.
  • Uniform Policy: Uniforms are mandatory, and employees should wear them during their work hours. In cases where a uniform isn't available, employees must maintain a proper dress code suitable for the work environment.
  • Attendance Tracking: Attendance will be recorded using both biometric systems and a register. Employees are required to ensure their daily attendance is accurately recorded.
  • Salary Calculation: Employee salaries will be credited based on their daily attendance. Regular attendance is vital for accurate compensation.
  • Mobile Device Usage: The use of mobile devices in the working area is strictly prohibited to maintain productivity and safety.
  • Punctuality Tolerance: While punctuality is crucial, a 5-minute grace period will be accepted in exceptional cases.
  • Substance Abuse: Substance abuse in the working area is strictly prohibited for the safety and well-being of all employees.
  • Late Entry Penalty: For late entry, a half-session salary deduction will be imposed after the issuance of three memos.
  • Leave Notification: If an employee needs to take leave, they must inform their Supervisor or Manager at least three days in advance, using the provided Leave Form. This ensures proper attendance management.
  • Emergency Communication: In case of emergencies, employees should contact and inform their Supervisor or Manager. However, this excuse is only valid for genuine emergencies. Failure to inform may result in a salary deduction ranging from Rs. 100 to Rs. 500.
  • Attendance Incentive: Employees who maintain 100% attendance will receive a monthly incentive of Rs. 500.
  • Uniform Charges on Early Resignation: In the event of resignation before completing three months of employment, uniform charges will be applied.
  • Safety Measures: Employees are required to dress appropriately for safety. This includes wearing shoes that cover the feet (no sandals) and avoiding loose-fitting clothing. The wearing of jewelry such as bracelets and rings should be avoided.

Additional Notes:

Discipline: Maintaining discipline is of utmost importance for all staff and employees to ensure a productive and harmonious work environment.

Uniform Exceptions: Exceptions to the uniform policy may be granted for one day per week.

Mobile Device Policy: Mobile devices must be handed over to the Supervisor before the commencement of work to prevent unauthorized use during working hours.

By joining the company, employees acknowledge and agree to abide by these terms and conditions. Failure to comply with these rules may result in appropriate actions and penalties as deemed necessary by the management.
